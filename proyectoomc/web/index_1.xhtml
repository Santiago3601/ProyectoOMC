About

Cryogas is a company of the group Air Products, one of the main companies of industrial gases globally, with more than 75 years of history and approximately 16.000 workers on 50 countries.

The company have a deep knowledge of every market which the company is present and contribute actively to the development with the companies that we work, thanks to the delivery of industrial solutions with gases and welds tailored to customers, which complements with a complete mix of products and services associated at the use of the them
